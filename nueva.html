BANAUE RICE TERRACES

The Banaue Rice Terraces are terraces 
that were carved into the mountains of Banaue, 
Ifugao, in the Philippines, by the ancestors of 
the Igorot people. The terraces are occasionally 
called the "Eighth Wonder of the World". It is 
commonly thought that the terraces were built with 
minimal equipment, largely by hand.
